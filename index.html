EFFECT OF A HIGH CARBON FOOTPRINT

Climate change:Increased greenhouse gas emissions trap more heat in the Earth's atmosphere, lead to global warming and climate change.
This causes extreme events droughys, hurricanes, and wildfires

Melting ice craps and Rising sea Levels:Global warming causes polar ice to melt, rising sea levels and threatening coastal communities with flooding.

Ecosystem Damage:Rising temperatures disrupt ecosystems and biodiversity.
Some species may face extinction due to habitat loss or altered condition

Health issues:Increasedair pollution from greenhouse gases can lead to respiratory problem,heart dieasesand other health issues for humans,especiallyin urban areas.

Economic Impact:Climate-related disasters can lead to high cost for recovery and adaptation.
Rising temperatures also affect agriculture,tourism and other sectors, potentially causing job and economic losses

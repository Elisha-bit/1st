SOME CAUSES OF CARBON FOOTPRINT

Some majior cause carbon footprint are as follows

Energy production:The burning of fossil fuels for electricity,heat and transportation is the primary source of carbon emissions.
Coal,oil and natural gas releasehigh level of co2

transportation:Cars,airplanes,ships,and trains that runon fossil fuels emit Co2 and other greenhouse gases.
Air travel, in particular,has a significant carbon footprint.

Industrial Activities:Manufacturing and construction activities release Co2 and other gases,especially those that rely on fossil fuel powered machinery and processes.

Agriculture:Agriculture activities release methane and nitrous oxide, both potent greenhouse gases.
Lvestock farming, rice cultivation, and the use of synthetic, fertilizers contribute to emissions

Deforestation:Cutting down forests for agriculture or urban development reduces the number of trees that can absorb Co2, increasing the amount of carbon in the atmosphere

Waste Management:Decomposition of organic waste in landfills produces methane.
Additionally, waste incineration release Co2

CARBON FOOTPRINT

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (including carbon dioxide and methane) that are generated by our actions

The average carbon footprint for a person in the united states is 16 tons, oneof the highest rate in the world.Globally, the average carbon footprint is closer to 4 tons.To have the best chance of avoiding a 2C rise in global temperatures,the average carbon footprint per year needs to drop to 2 tons by 2050

Lowering indivdual carbon footprints from 16 ton to 2 ton doesn't happen overnight! By making small changes to our actions, like eating less meat, taking fewer connecting flights and line drying our clothes,we can start making a big difference.
